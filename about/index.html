Welcome to The Sky Travels

At Sky Travel Service, we specialize in offering reliable and comprehensive travel and visa solutions tailored to meet your needs. Established with the vision of simplifying global travel and migration processes, we have built a reputation for integrity, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.We take pride in being a trusted partner for individuals, families, and businesses seeking seamless and efficient travel-related services

Explore With Us — Trusted Visa & Travel Experts Since 1993

Established in 1993 and registered under Regd-BOM/PART/4000+/5/3902/1993, Sky Travel Service is a government-approved manpower consultancy and visa facilitation agency committed to delivering professional and reliable travel solutions. Over the years, we have built a trusted name in the industry by consistently providing accurate, timely, and personalized support to our clients.

With our Head Office in Mumbai and our Administrative Office in Calicut, Kerala, we are well-positioned to serve customers across India and abroad. Our experienced team ensures every aspect of your travel and visa process is handled with the utmost care and precision.
